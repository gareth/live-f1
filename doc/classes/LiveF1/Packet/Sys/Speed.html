Class LiveF1::Packet::Sys::Speed
In: lib/live-f1/packet.rb
Parent: Sys

Speed packets contain information about the top 6 recorded driver speeds through the 4 speed traps on the lap (at the end of each of the 3 sectors, and at the dedicated speed trap)

driver:The 3-letter driver abbrevaition
